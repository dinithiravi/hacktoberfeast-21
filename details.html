Description and Services

Located on the hills of Nice, a short distance from the famous Promenade des Anglais, Hotel ABC is one of the hotels in the Costa Azzurra (or Blue Coast) able to satisfy the different needs of its guests with comfort and first rate services. It is only 2 km from the airport and from highway exits. The hotel has a large parking area , a real luxury in a city like Nice..

At Hotel ABC you will be welcomed amongst olive trees, citron trees and magnolias, in gardens that hide exotic plants and in a wonderful outdoor pool with deck chairs; protected against the sun’s rays by big umbrellas you can enjoy a drink amongst the wisteria bushes, and find peace and tranquillity enjoying Costa Azzurra’s mild climate.
